FAQs.

  • Is the FREE package free forever?
  • Yes. The free package is forever. It is intended for experimentation, but is definitely suitable for those who have a small number of students.
  • Does the subscription renew automatically?
  • Yes. Subscription to standard and premium packages is automatically renewed once a month.
  • What happens after cancelling the subscription?
  • You will no longer be able to enter NextTutor application (web + mobile). If the subscription is not renewed in a month, the account will be closed permanentlly and all information related to your account will be removed from our servers.
  • Why do I need a Google account?
  • Our servers do not store files. They will save in your GOOGLE DRIVE account. If you choose to cancel the subscription for any reason, all the files you created will remain with you. (because they are yours!)
  • Is there a limit on the number of lessons/offline tasks I can give?
  • There is no limit to the number of lessons and tasks you can give. On the contrary, we are here to help you giving as many lessons and offline tasks as possible and maximize your profit.
